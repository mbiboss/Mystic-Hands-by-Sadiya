Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my henna session?

Your session will begin with a design consultation, followed by the application process. The paste needs to stay on for 4-6 hours for the best results. We recommend arriving with clean, oil-free skin for optimal staining.

How long does the henna stain last?

The stain typically lasts 1-3 weeks depending on aftercare and placement. Peak color is usually achieved 48 hours after application. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to help maintain your design.

Are your henna products safe?

Yes, we use 100% natural henna powder mixed with essential oils and natural ingredients. All our products are carefully sourced, tested, and safe for skin application. We never use chemical dyes or black henna.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes, we offer special packages for group bookings, perfect for bridal parties, festivals, and corporate events. Our group services include customized designs and competitive pricing for parties of 5 or more.

What's your booking and cancellation policy?

We require a 25% deposit to secure your booking. Cancellations made 48 hours before the appointment receive a full refund. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Do you travel for events?

Yes, we offer mobile services for events within the city limits. For destinations beyond, additional travel charges may apply. Contact us for a custom quote based on your location and requirements.
