The Artist Behind the Art

Sadiya's Journey

Born into a family of traditional artists, Sadiya began practicing henna at age 12 under her grandmother's guidance. What started as a cultural tradition evolved into a lifelong passion.

After completing her Fine Arts degree at Dhaka University, Sadiya traveled across South Asia and the Middle East, studying regional henna techniques and building her unique style.
