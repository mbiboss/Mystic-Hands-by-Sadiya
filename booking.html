Frequently Asked Questions

How long does henna last?

Henna typically lasts 1-3 weeks depending on the area applied, your skin type, and how well you care for it. Hand designs usually last 7-10 days, while feet designs can last longer.

When should I book for a bridal henna?

For bridal henna, I recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance to secure your date. The actual application should be done 2-3 days before your wedding for the best color on your special day.

Is your henna safe for everyone?

My henna paste is 100% natural and made from organic ingredients. It's safe for most people, but I recommend a patch test if you have sensitive skin. I do not use black henna or any chemical additives.

Do you travel for appointments?

Yes, I offer mobile services within a 30-mile radius of central London. Travel fees may apply depending on the distance. For bridal services, I always come to your location.

How can I make my henna darker?

For the darkest stain, keep the paste on for at least 4-6 hours, avoid water for 24 hours after removal, and apply a mixture of lemon juice and sugar after the paste comes off. I'll provide detailed aftercare instructions.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any service and are valid for one year from the purchase date. They make wonderful gifts for special occasions.
